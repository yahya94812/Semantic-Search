# Japan Trip Planning — April 2024

**Duration:** 14 days
**Travelling with:** Maya

## Itinerary Draft

### Days 1–3: Tokyo

- Arrive at Narita, take Narita Express to Shinjuku.
- **Stay:** Shinjuku area (booked Keio Plaza Hotel, ¥18,000/night).
- **Visit:** Shibuya crossing, Meiji Shrine, Harajuku, Akihabara.
- **Food:** Try tsukemen at Fuunji, yakitori at Yurakucho alley.
- **Day trip:** Nikko Toshogu shrine via Tobu line.

### Days 4–5: Hakone

- Mt Fuji views from Hakone Open Air Museum.
- Ryokan stay with onsen (booked Gora Kadan — splurge!).
- Ride the Romancecar from Shinjuku.

### Days 6–8: Kyoto

- Shinkansen from Odawara to Kyoto (~2h).
- **Stay:** Gion district guesthouse.
- Fushimi Inari gates at sunrise (beat the crowds).
- Arashiyama bamboo grove and Tenryu-ji garden.
- Nishiki Market for street food.
- **Day trip** to Nara (deer park + Todai-ji temple).

### Days 9–10: Osaka

- 40-min train from Kyoto.
- Dotonbori for takoyaki and okonomiyaki.
- Osaka Castle in the morning.
- **Day trip** to Himeji Castle (worth it!).

### Days 11–12: Hiroshima & Miyajima

- Peace Memorial Museum (allow 3 hours, bring tissues).
- Miyajima island — see the floating torii gate at high tide.
- Try Hiroshima-style okonomiyaki (different from Osaka version).

### Days 13–14: Back to Tokyo

- Shinkansen return, final shopping in Shibuya.
- Fly home from Haneda (cheaper taxi than Narita).

## Packing Notes

- Bring cash — many places still card-unfriendly.
- IC card (Suica) for trains and convenience stores.
- Portable WiFi pre-booked via IIJmio.
- Pack light — coin laundry everywhere.
